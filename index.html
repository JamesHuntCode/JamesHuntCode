About James Hunt

James is currently studying BSc(Hons) Computing at the University of Plymouth. At the moment James is completing his first year aiming to finish with a 1st.

James is originally from Plymouth, United Kingdom with family based in the United States of America. As well as studying for his degree in Computing, James is also the Chairman & Co-Founder of the Plymouth University Computer/Computing Society. James also volunteers weekly at Plymouth Central Library to help out with 'Hello World', a coding club aimed at young children. This is because James recognises the importance of introducing code and technology to a younger audience.

James has been programming incessantly over the last five years, often making websites and remaking arcade games he played during his childhood.

James currently shows significant interest in desktop application development and client side web scripting.
